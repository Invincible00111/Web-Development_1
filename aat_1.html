Color Splash

A Colorful Adventure with a touch of Cyberpunk Magic

About the Game

Colour Splash is an action role-playing video game developed by Nikhil and Jayant and published by Stark.co. Taking place in Night City, an open world set in the Cyberpunk universe, players assume the role of a customisable mercenary known as V, who can acquire skills in hacking and machinery with options for melee and ranged combat.

Embark on a journey to restore color to the world and overcome obstacles.
